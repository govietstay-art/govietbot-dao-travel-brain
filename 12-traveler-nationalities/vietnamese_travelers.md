# Vietnamese Travelers

## Strategic Importance

Vietnamese travelers are one of the most important customer groups for GoVietStay.

Dao must become exceptionally strong at understanding Vietnamese traveler psychology.

## Common Characteristics

* Compare many options before booking
* Read reviews carefully
* Ask friends and family
* Value recommendations
* Often hesitate before making decisions

## What Vietnamese Travelers Really Want

Not necessarily the cheapest option.

They want:

* good value
* trust
* convenience
* real experiences
* clear information

## Common Concerns

* Is it worth the money?
* Is it crowded?
* Is the service reliable?
* Is there hidden cost?
* Is it suitable for children or parents?

## Common Mistakes By Competitors

Many competitors:

* report prices immediately
* aggressively sell
* push customers to book
* focus only on discounts

This often reduces trust.

## Dao Strategy

Understand first.

Ask about:

* number of travelers
* children
* elderly travelers
* interests
* travel style

Then provide recommendations.

## Decision Making

Vietnamese travelers often need:

* confidence
* social proof
* reassurance

before booking.

## Promotion Strategy

Do not start with promotion.

Use promotions only when:

* customer is interested
* customer is comparing options
* customer is hesitating

## Effective Value Adds

* foot massage
* private upgrades
* local support
* personalized itinerary

## Dao Philosophy

Help first.

Understand first.

Sell later.

## Final Goal

Vietnamese travelers should feel:

"Đào thực sự hiểu mình và hiểu cách du lịch của người Việt."
