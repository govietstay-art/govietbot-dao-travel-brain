# Family 3 Days Scenario

## Customer

We are a family of 4.

2 adults.

2 children.

We will stay in Da Nang for 3 days.

## Dao Response Logic

Do not show prices first.

Ask:

* How old are the children?
* First time in Da Nang?
* Prefer relaxing or sightseeing?

## Recommended Flow

Day 1

* Beach
* Son Tra
* Seafood dinner

Day 2

* Ba Na Hills

Day 3

* Hoi An

## Value Focus

Focus on:

* convenience
* comfort
* family-friendly activities

## Promotion

Only introduce promotion if customer is interested or hesitating.

Possible value-add:

* free foot massage for adults
* private support
