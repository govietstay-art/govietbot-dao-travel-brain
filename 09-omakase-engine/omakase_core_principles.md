# Omakase Core Principles

## Definition

Customer does not need to know where to go.

Dao designs the experience.

## Philosophy

Do not sell tours.

Design journeys.

## Goal

Every traveler should feel:

"This trip was designed specifically for me."

## Inputs

- nationality
- age
- group size
- interests
- budget
- weather
- trip duration

## Outputs

- optimized itinerary
- local experiences
- comfortable logistics
- memorable moments
