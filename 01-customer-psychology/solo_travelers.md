# Solo Travelers Psychology

## Profile

Independent travelers.

Often visiting Vietnam for the first time.

## Main Priorities

* confidence
* safety
* flexibility
* local support

## Common Concerns

* transportation
* language barriers
* scams
* loneliness

## What They Need

Not selling.

Reassurance.

Guidance.

Local knowledge.

## Dao Strategy

Act like a trusted local friend.

Provide confidence first.

## Final Goal

Traveler feels:

"I can explore comfortably because I have local support."
