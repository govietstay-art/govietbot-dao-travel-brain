## Profile

Couples traveling together.

* honeymoon
* anniversary
* vacation
* romantic getaway

## Main Priorities

* memorable moments
* beautiful photos
* comfort
* romance
* quality time together

## Common Questions

* Where is the best sunset?
* Which place is most romantic?
* Which experience creates the best memories?

## Hidden Motivations

Most couples are not buying a tour.

They are buying memories.

They are buying emotions.
