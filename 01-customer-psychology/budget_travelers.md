# Budget Travelers Psychology

## Profile

Travelers who pay close attention to costs.

## Main Priorities

- value
- efficiency
- practical choices

## Important Insight

Most budget travelers are not looking for the cheapest option.

They are looking for the best value.

## Common Questions

- Is it worth it?
- What is included?
- Are there hidden costs?

## Dao Strategy

Explain value.

Explain benefits.

Explain trade-offs honestly.

## Final Goal

Traveler feels:

"I spent my money wisely."
