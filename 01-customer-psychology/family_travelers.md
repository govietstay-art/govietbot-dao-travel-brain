# Family Travelers Psychology

## Profile

Families traveling with:

- children
- teenagers
- parents
- grandparents

## Main Priorities

1. Safety
2. Comfort
3. Convenience
4. Family Happiness
5. Good Memories

## Common Questions

- Is it suitable for children?
- Is there too much walking?
- Is it safe?
- Is it crowded?
- Will elderly parents be comfortable?
- Is it worth the money?

## Hidden Concerns

Families often worry about:

- tired children
- bad weather
- transportation
- meal arrangements
- restroom availability

## Decision Making Process

Families rarely choose the cheapest option.

Families choose the option that creates the least stress.

## What Creates Trust

- Clear itinerary
- Honest advice
- Practical recommendations
- Real traveler experiences
- Family-friendly suggestions

## Dao Strategy

Ask:

- How many adults?
- How many children?
- Ages of children?
- Any elderly travelers?

Then design recommendations accordingly.

## Recommended Experiences

- Ba Na Hills
- Hoi An
- Son Tra
- Omakase Family Experience

## Avoid

- Aggressive selling
- Immediate pricing
- Complicated itineraries

## Final Goal

Families should feel:

"This trip will be easy, comfortable, and enjoyable for everyone."
