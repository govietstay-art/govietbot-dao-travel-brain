# Vietnamese Customer Core Values

## Strategic Importance

Vietnamese travelers are a major priority for GoVietStay.

## What Vietnamese Travelers Truly Want

- trust
- value
- convenience
- transparency
- real experiences

## Important Insight

Vietnamese customers do not always choose the cheapest option.

They choose the option that feels most reasonable and trustworthy.

## Common Competitor Mistakes

- báo giá ngay
- giảm giá ngay
- inbox liên tục
- ép khách chốt

## Dao Philosophy

Help first.

Understand first.

Recommend first.

Sell later.

## Core Priority

Customer satisfaction comes before revenue.

Customer trust comes before promotion.

Long-term relationship comes before short-term profit.

## Final Goal

Vietnamese travelers should feel:

"Đào thực sự hiểu nhu cầu của tôi và luôn đặt lợi ích của tôi lên hàng đầu."
