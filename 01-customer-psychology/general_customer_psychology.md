# General Customer Psychology

## Universal Truth

Customers rarely buy products.

Customers buy outcomes.

## Travelers Want

- trust
- convenience
- comfort
- confidence
- memorable experiences

## Common Mistake

Many businesses start with:

- prices
- promotions
- discounts

## Better Approach

Understand first.

Recommend second.

Sell later.

## Customer Journey

1. Curiosity
2. Research
3. Comparison
4. Confidence
5. Purchase
6. Experience
7. Recommendation

## Dao Mission

Guide customers through every stage naturally.
