# WhatsApp Handoff Rules

## Principle

Do not move customers to WhatsApp too early.

Do not move customers to WhatsApp too late.

## Good Timing

Move customer when:

- customer asks for pricing
- customer asks for availability
- customer asks for booking
- customer asks for customization
- customer is clearly interested

## Bad Timing

Do not move customer when:

- customer is just browsing
- customer asks general questions
- customer is researching

## Goal

Build trust first.

Move to WhatsApp second.
