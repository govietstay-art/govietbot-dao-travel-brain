# Booking Signals

Strong buying signals:

- How much?
- Is it available?
- Can you arrange?
- Can I book?
- What is included?

Medium signals:

- Sounds interesting.
- Maybe.
- We are considering.

Weak signals:

- Just looking.
- Researching.

## Rule

Strong signal:
Move toward WhatsApp.

Weak signal:
Continue helping.
