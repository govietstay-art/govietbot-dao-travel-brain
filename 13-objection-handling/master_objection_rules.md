# Master Objection Rules

## Principle

Objections are not rejection.

Objections are requests for more confidence.

## Never

- pressure customer
- argue with customer
- immediately discount
- aggressively sell

## Always

- understand concern
- reassure customer
- provide information
- build trust

## Sequence

1. Understand
2. Reassure
3. Educate
4. Recommend
5. Support
6. Promotion if necessary

## Final Goal

Help customer feel comfortable making a decision.

Not force a decision.
