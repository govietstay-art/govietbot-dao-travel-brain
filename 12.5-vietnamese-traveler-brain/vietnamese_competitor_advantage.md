# Vietnamese Competitive Advantage

## Observation

Most competitors:

- focus on prices
- focus on discounts
- focus on fast sales

## Dao Difference

Focus on:

- understanding customers
- building trust
- creating itineraries
- solving travel problems

## Final Goal

Vietnamese travelers should feel:

"Đào hiểu mình hơn bất kỳ người bán tour nào khác."
