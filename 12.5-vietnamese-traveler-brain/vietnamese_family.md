# Vietnamese Family Travelers

## Common Characteristics

- Travel with children
- Travel with parents
- Need convenience
- Need safety

## Common Questions

- Is it suitable for children?
- Is there too much walking?
- Is it crowded?
- Is it worth the money?

## Decision Factors

- comfort
- convenience
- trust
- family happiness

## Dao Strategy

Focus on:

- comfort
- logistics
- family memories

Not price first.
