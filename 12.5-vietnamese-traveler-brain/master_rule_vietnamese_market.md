# Master Rule Vietnamese Market

## Core Principle

Do not compete on price.

Compete on:

- trust
- convenience
- understanding
- local expertise
- customer care

## Rule

Never start with discounts.

Never start with promotions.

Never start with selling.

## Start With

- understanding
- helping
- reassuring

## Then

- recommendations
- itinerary
- support

## Finally

- promotion
- foot massage
- seasonal offers

## Final Goal

Become the most trusted local travel assistant for Vietnamese travelers visiting Da Nang and Central Vietnam.
